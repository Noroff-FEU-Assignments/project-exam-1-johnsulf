About

PuttProfessor

Welcome to the about section of puttingLAB, your ultimate source for mastering the art of putting in disc golf. Let's get to know the man behind it all, the one and only PuttProfessor.

Who is PuttProfessor?

PuttProfessor is an experienced disc golfer with over 10 years of teaching and competitive play under his belt. With a passion for the sport and an analytical mind, he has dissected the art of putting down to a science.

Why puttingLAB?

In disc golf, a great drive is nothing without an accurate putt to finish it off. Understanding the mechanics, psychology, and equipment that go into every successful putt is what PuttProfessor aims to share with you in the puttingLAB.

What Can You Learn Here?

  • Techniques: From push putts to spin putts, we've got it covered.
  • Equipment: Get reviews and tips on choosing the best disc and other equipment.
  • Psychology: Learn how to keep your focus under pressure.
  • Community: Stay updated on the latest events and connect with fellow disc golf enthusiasts.
